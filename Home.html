Featured Trips

Witness History at the Citadel of Qaitbay

Experience the grandeur of this 15th-century fortress, offering stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea.1 Our expert guides will delve into its rich history and strategic importance.

Step Back in Time at the Roman Theater

Immerse yourself in the ancient world as you explore this well-preserved amphitheater. Our knowledgeable guides will bring the past to life with stories of gladiators and theatrical performances.

Step into Royalty at Montazah Palace

Experience the opulence of the Egyptian royal family at this stunning seaside palace. Admire the beautiful gardens, ornate architecture, and breathtaking views of the Mediterranean Sea. Our knowledgeable guides will share stories of the palace's history and the royal family who once called it home.

Uncover Ancient Wonders at the Egyptian Museum

Immerse yourself in the heart of Egyptian history at this world-renowned museum. Marvel at the treasures of the pharaohs, including Tutankhamun's golden mask and mummies dating back thousands of years. Our expert guides will bring these ancient artifacts to life, sharing captivating stories and insights into the mysteries of the past.

Immerse Yourself in the Magic of Khan el-Khalili

Lose yourself in the vibrant colors and aromatic scents of this historic bazaar. Our expert guides will lead you through its winding alleyways, introducing you to skilled artisans and helping you find unique treasures.

Cruise the Majestic Nile River

Embark on a serene journey along the Nile River, the lifeblood of Egypt. Admire the stunning landscapes, ancient temples, and charming villages that line its banks. Our expert guides will share fascinating stories about the river's history and cultural significance.

Unwind on the Pristine Beaches of the Red Sea

Escape the hustle and bustle and relax on the pristine beaches of the Red Sea. Our expert guides will arrange unforgettable snorkeling and diving excursions, allowing you to explore vibrant coral reefs and encounter fascinating marine life.

Experience the Vibrant Nightlife of Soho Square

Immerse yourself in the lively atmosphere of Soho Square, Sharm El-Sheikh's premier entertainment district. Dance the night away at world-class clubs, savor delicious cuisine at chic restaurants, and enjoy thrilling water shows. Our expert guides will help you navigate the square's diverse offerings, ensuring an unforgettable night out.
