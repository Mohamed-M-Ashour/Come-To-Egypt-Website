Full-Day Hurghada Sea Escape: Diving, Water Sports, Island Bliss, and Gourmet Dining

The first stop was an incredible dive into crystal-clear waters, revealing vibrant coral reefs and colorful marine life. A second dive followed, equally breathtaking.
